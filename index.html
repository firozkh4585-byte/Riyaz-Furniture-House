Project Proposal

The proposed project is to develop and host a professional website for Riyaz Furniture House, a family-owned furniture business. The website will showcase a wide range of furniture products including sofas, beds wardrobes, dining tables, office furniture and home decor items. Customers will be able to browse available products, view descriptions and prices, and easily contact the business for enquiries or orders.

The website is designed to improve the online presence of Riyaz Furniture House and make it easier for customers to discover our products from anywhere. It will include business information, contact details, showroom location, product categories and high-quality images of furniture. This will help increase customer trust, attract new buyers and promote the business beyond the local area.

The website will be hosted on an Infrastructure as a Service model using Microsoft Azure to ensure reliabilty, security, and Scalability. Azure's robust infrastruture will provide the necessary resources and tools to handle varying maintain high availability, traffic loads, and protect against cyber threats. This will include load balancing to distribute traffic evenly, auto-scaling to accommodate traffic spikes, and advanced security measures like firewalls, DDoS protection and regular backups to safeguard data integrity.

The intended users of this website are homeowners, businesses, interior designers and anyone looking to purchase quality furniture. By creating this website, Riyaz Furniture House will improve its digital presence, provide better cuatomer service and expand its business opportunities.
